What is OpenSlate

Every Tool. One Suite.

Everything a filmmaker needs from the first draft to the last shot — completely free.

Write Your Script

Industry-standard screenplay formatting with smart autocomplete and a real-time Script Doctor that flags professional issues as you write.

Plan Your Shoot

Full pre-production suite — storyboard every shot, build your shot list, manage your cast and locations, schedule your crew.

Ship Your Film

Export clean PDFs at every stage. From formatted screenplay to production call sheet — professional output with one click.
